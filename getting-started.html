Angular Buddies FOSS hackday!

The goal of the FOSS hackday is like an Amish barn raising - lots of folks getting together to accomplish a great thing in a day.

Requirements

You're only required to have three things: A non-zero level of JavaScript knowledge, a GitHub account, & a willingness to ask questions. The hackday is for programmers of all skill levels. Even if you're just starting out, we want you to succeed.

Getting started

  • Join the Angular Buddies Slack group here: angularbuddies.com
  • Join the #foss-bash channel
